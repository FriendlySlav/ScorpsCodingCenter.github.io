Scorps Coding Center

Article I - Name, Purpose, and Authority The name of the club shall be Scorps Computing Center (SCC) of Adolfo Camarillo High School, The organization's goals will be to create a welcoming environment for new computer programmers where they can discuss computer science problems which are used in the interviews of the biggest tech companies in the world such as Amazon, Google, Facebook, etc. This club/organization will also provide many opportunities for all levels of developers to join a junior developing team and gain experience as a team and as a contributing individual in some of the club's large upcoming computer science projects. Article II - Membership All students enrolled in Adolfo Camarillo High School are eligible for membership. Article III - Meetings Meetings will be held during the second and fourth Thursday of each month during lunch unless otherwise specified by the club president or vice president. Article IV - Club Officers The club should consist of the following: President Vice-President Treasurer Secretary Article V - Duties The club President shall have the following duties: To preside over meetings of the club. To call special meetings of the club. To plan an agenda for the club and the meetings. The club Vice-President shall have the following duties: To serve as the club President of the current President is unable to fulfill their duties temporarily or permanently. To support the club, President. The club treasurer will have the following duties: To maintain complete and accurate records of all Club receipts and disbursements. To oversee club fund-raising activities. To supervise the preparation of the club budget The club secretary will have the following duties: To maintain accurate minutes of each meeting. To carry out all correspondence for the club. Article VI - Elections The club will hold an election of officers once a year. The voting will take place by anonymous ballot. Names of officers will be recorded with the student council. Article VII - Amendment A two-thirds majority ruling of members in attendance is required to amend this club constitution.
