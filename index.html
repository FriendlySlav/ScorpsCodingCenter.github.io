Scorps Coding Center

The name of the club is Scorps Computing Center (SCC) of Adolfo Camarillo High School, The organization's goals is to create a welcoming environment for new computer programmers where they can discuss computer science problems which are used in the interviews of the biggest tech companies in the world such as Amazon, Google, Facebook, etc. This club/organization also provides many opportunities for all levels of developers to join a junior developing team to gain experienceas a contributing individual in some of the club's large upcoming computer science projects.
